Breezy

Fake Notifications

Description


Invoke fake notifications with custom titles, message and app of your choice using 5 different gestures.


Title

You can write your own title from Breezy's settings


Message

You can write your own message from Breezy's settings


Apps

You can choose which apps you want a fake notification show from


Gestures

You can choose either double tap on the statusbar, double tap on the dock, press volume up/down or shake your device to invoke the fake notification
