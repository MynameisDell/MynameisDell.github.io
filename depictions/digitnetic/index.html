Digitnetic

Floating keypad & calculator



Watch promo video

Description


Sometimes we need to call a certain phone number VERY quickly, however you might not have enough time to unlock your device, scroll through SpringBoard to find the Phone app, dial the phone number then call it. Digitnetic brings a beautiful floating phone keypad to your device that can be invoked by shaking your device, you can have access to it whenever you need to, so this way would be much easier for you to dial the phone number and call that number. Digitnetic doesn’t only include a phone keypad, it also includes a calculator to your device aswell, you can switch between the phone keypad or calculator, it’s useful for someone who needs to use the calculator to solve math problems.


Gestures

Shake your device to show Digitnetic, long press to show the menu where you can switch between the keypad or calculator and hide digitnetic.


Double tap on the Calculator result field to copy the it.


Double tap on the keypad number field to paste the phone number.


Appearance

You can choose between light, dark or dynamic mode, if you don’t want to use those then you can colour Digitnetic to your liking.
