Nova

Schedule Messages



Watch promo video

Description


Nova brings iMessage/SMS scheduling to iOS 14 with easy to use UI, Yes it’s very easy to set scheduled iMessages/SMS. The point of Nova is, if you are extremely busy with your day to day life when it’s either, work, school, college, university or any other events, you can set a scheduled message while you are doing your routine then it will send the message automatically at a date and time of your choice. If you have a friend or family member who lives oversea’s and at different time-zone, you can send scheduled messages at 3am while you are sleeping so the recipient will receive a message at 10am exactly, for their time-zone. It’s very useful for someone who wants to send a happy birthday message or an important message to remind someone to attend their doctors appointment. Nova’s sleek and unobtrusive design allows it to fit in seamlessly with the Messages app, end-user experience is very important to us, we have carefully designed Nova from preferences to tweaks, made it more user-friendly so it won’t be confusing or complicated to set up a scheduled message and you would feel like you are actually in a chat with a recipient when composing a message.


Contacts

It’s using an avatar image you added in your contact list, if you want to use a custom image then you can assign a custom avatar image when creating the scheduled message


Sections

There are 3 sections to show scheduled messages, Today, Future and Sent


Attachment

You can send up to 5 image attachments with each scheduled message


Schedule Button

You can set the floating button alignment to left, centre and right. The button will fade away when you scroll down the conversation list and appear again once you scroll to the top.


Appearance

Nova can adapt your system setting dark or light mode, if you don’t want to use system appearance then you can colour everything to your liking so it will fit in with any set up.


Settings

All settings can be configured from the Settings or Messages app


Pricing

We are offering a 2 day free trial which can be activated from Nova’s preferences pane, you will have full access to all of Nova’s features and explore more whether Nova is for you or not, when the trial period is over, you can decide if you want to purchase Nova. If you don’t want to use the 2 day free trial, you can purchase Nova from preferences, the process is very simple, you can make a payment via Stripe or PayPal and once your payment is completed then Nova will be activated automatically.
